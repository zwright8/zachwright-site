What AgenticCoding means on this site

AgenticCoding is my practical method for translating workflow pain into production-grade AI operations. I focus on where teams actually lose time and context, then design agent behavior and control points that hold up in production.

The target is measurable operating lift, not demo-level novelty. Every implementation includes baseline metrics, owner mapping, and escalation logic.

  • Map workflow friction and high-leverage automation opportunities
  • Define permissions, approvals, and fail-safe behavior
  • Ship with observability and weekly optimization cycles
